Bio-inspired Autonomous Fish Robot


Our motivation to take up this project is to aid underwater computer vision by fabricating a robust fish robot with a camera that can capture underwater environments. This project is a collaboration with IEEE NITK under the guidance of Prof. Guruprasad KR and will be presented in the NITK Project Expo 2019.

We want to develop a fish robot using the concepts of soft-robotics and visual servoing to rendevouz with underwater targets. The fish will be actuated by 2 servo motors, sense the environment with an IMU sensor and a camera and be controlled by a Raspberry Pi.

My Contributions Highlights:

  • Developped the dynamic equations of the mechanical motion
  • Leading a team of 5 members to implement and test different sub-sections of overall system which includes the computer program, electrical circuitry and mechanical assembly
